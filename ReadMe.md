<h2>Keybinding Setup</h2>

<h3>Movement</h3>
<p>
Up [W] <br>
Down [S] <br>
Left [A] <br>
Right [D]
</p>
<h3>Attack</h3>
<p>
Attack [SPACE] <br>
Switch weapons [R] <br>
Spells [LSHIFT] <br>
Switch spells [Q] <br>
</p>
<h3>Menu</h3>
<p>
Upgrade menu [M] or [ESC]<br>
Switching in menu [Left arrow] and [Right arrow] <br>
Select in upgrade menu [SPACE]
</p>
